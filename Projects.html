Plant monitoring system

Plant monitoring system helps you to ensure that the your plant is getting enough sunlight and water for it's proper growth. This system uses multiple sensors to sense amount of sunlight, humidity, temperature and etc. It can give you a report of all these parameters w.r.t. time. It can also give you an alert in case your plant has any issues.

Bitcoin price alert System

Bitcoin price alert system gives you an alert whenever the price of bitcoin crosses the threshold value set by you. It uses Bolt WiFi module for giving an alert to your mobile phone through text message and email. This also gives you an alert on buzzer connected with WiFi module.

email classifier

Email classifier is machine learning based project which can classify email as SPAM or not based on the text of an Email. This is implemented in MATLAB/Octave. It uses Support Vector machines (Supervised learning algorithm). It can accurately help you to decide whether an email you recieved is spam or not.
